AVERAGE: Calculating the Mean

The **AVERAGE** function calculates the **arithmetic mean** (the average) of the numbers in a range of cells. It does this by adding the values and dividing by the count of numeric values.

**Important:** AVERAGE **ignores empty cells** and cells containing **text**. It only calculates the mean of numerical values.

---

Formula Syntax

=AVERAGE(number1, [number2], ...)
---

Arguments (Parts of the Formula)

  • number1: The first number or range you want to average. This is required. (e.g., a single score or the range `C1:C20`).
  • [number2], ...: *Optional*. Additional numbers or ranges, up to 255 total, that you want to include in the average calculation.
---

Example üí°

To find the average score for all students listed in cells D2 through D50:

=AVERAGE(D2:D50)
