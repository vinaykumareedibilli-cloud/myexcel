SUM: Adding Numbers in a Range

The **SUM** function is one of the most basic and frequently used Excel formulas. It simply **adds all the numbers** in a specified range of cells.

**Key Use:** Calculating grand totals, inventory counts, or cumulative scores.

---

Formula Syntax

=SUM(number1, [number2], ...)
---

Arguments (Parts of the Formula)

  • number1: The first number or range you want to add. This is required. (e.g., `A1` or `B2:B10`).
  • [number2], ...: *Optional*. Additional numbers or ranges, up to 255 total, that you want to include in the sum.
---

Example üí°

To find the total sales from cells A1 through A10 and add the sales total from cell C5:

=SUM(A1:A10, C5)
