SUMIF: Conditional Summation (Single Criterion)

The **SUMIF** function adds the values in a range that meet a specific single **criterion**. It's essential for calculating totals based on simple conditions like department, region, or status.

**Key Use:** Quickly summing sales figures for a specific product or finding the total cost for items marked "Urgent."
